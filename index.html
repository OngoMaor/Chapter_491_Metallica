About Us

We are a Metallica fan community in Israel.
Our only requirement is to love the band, and the goal is to unite all Metallica fans in Israel in one place, and of course bring the band back to the country.
If you want to be part of the fan club,
send us your first name + last name in English and your username as Fifth Members and you will automatically become part of the club. It's free!
